abstract: "“Adapt or Disappear: Future-proof Your Business with AI” is a multi-industry seminar designed to help businesses understand practical AI adoption—from creativity and engineering to finance and large-scale operations. Featuring leaders from NVIDIA Indonesia, DANA, tiket.com, and emerging AI-driven startups, the event explores how organizations can integrate AI to accelerate productivity, unlock new capabilities, and stay resilient in a rapidly changing market. I will join the panel to share insights from Konstruksi.AI, highlighting how AI transforms construction workflows and decision-making processes. The session aims to provide actionable strategies, real case studies, and a cross-industry perspective on using AI as a competitive advantage. The event is open to the public and will be held on Thursday, 6 November 2025, at the tiket.com office, Slipi."
